Television Energy Consumption

In Australia, TVs must display energy rating labels. LED and OLED TVs are far more energy-efficient compared to older plasma models.

Tips to Save Energy

  • Choose TVs with higher star ratings
  • Reduce screen brightness
  • Turn off when not in use
